About SmartCore LLC

Who we are

SmartCore LLC is a privately held limited liability company registered in the State of South Dakota, United States. We design, build, and ship desktop AI software with a focus on privacy, performance, and a respectful relationship with the user's machine.

What we believe

AI software belongs on the user's device, under the user's control. We build native Windows applications that do not phone home, do not ingest user data into third-party clouds, and do not bury surveillance in the install pipeline. The user owns the workflow end to end.

How we operate

We are a small, focused team. Software is built in-house, signed with an Extended Validation certificate from a recognised certificate authority, and distributed through audited channels. We publish a clear privacy policy and stand behind the software we ship.
