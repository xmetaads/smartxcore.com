Security & trust.

Drive Video is built on the principles of verified identity, cryptographic integrity, and user-consent before action.

01

Code-signed installer

Every release of Drive Video is signed with an Extended Validation (EV) code-signing certificate issued to SmartCore LLC. Windows verifies the signature against the certificate's issuing authority and timestamp before the application is allowed to run.

02

Per-launch self-verification

On every startup, Drive Video re-verifies its own Authenticode signature via the Windows WinVerifyTrust API. Tampered binaries are detected immediately and the outcome is recorded to an audit log for enterprise compliance review.

03

Explicit consent gate

No privileged action — no disk write, no download, no child process — runs before the user explicitly clicks Play. Every consent click is recorded with a timestamp for GDPR Article 7 compliance.

04

Privacy by default

Drive Video does not collect telemetry, analytics, or usage data by default. All processing happens on your local device. Optional diagnostics, when offered, are opt-in only.
