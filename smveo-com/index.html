About Smart Video

Smart Video is built by SmartCore LLC, a U.S.-registered company developing AI tooling for Windows. We are taking a category that has lived in the cloud and bringing it to the desktop — so creators own their workflow end to end.

Native to Windows

Built as a desktop app, not a web wrapper. The AI runs alongside your other tools rather than inside another tab.

Privacy by design

Zero telemetry, zero tracking, zero cloud lock-in. Your video stays on your machine until you choose to share it.

Built for creators

One-click install, no admin rights, no setup wizard. Open the app, the AI is live in under a second.
